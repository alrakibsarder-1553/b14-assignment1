Frequently Asked Questions

Will conference recordings be available later?

Yes! Pro and Team passes include lifetime access to all recordings. Standard pass holders will have access for 30 days after the event.

Are there any discounts for groups or teams?

Absolutely! Our 'Team Pass' allows you to bring up to 10 members together at a much lower cost per person compared to individual passes.

Can I refund or cancel my ticket later?

You can request a full refund up to 7 days before the conference starts. After that, tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred to someone else.

Will I receive a certificate of attendance?

Yes, a verified digital certificate will be sent to your registered email address after successfully completing the workshops and full conference.
